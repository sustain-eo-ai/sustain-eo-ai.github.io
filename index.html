Sustainability with Earth Observation & AI

An ICCV 2025 Workshop exploring innovative AI solutions for a sustainable future through Earth observation.

Introduction

The workshop brings together researchers, practitioners, and policy‑makers to advance the state‑of‑the‑art in applying artificial intelligence to Earth observation for sustainability challenges. Topics include climate monitoring, disaster response, biodiversity, agriculture, urban development, clean energy, and cross‑sensor data fusion.
